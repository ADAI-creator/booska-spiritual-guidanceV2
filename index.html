The Process

How Spiritual Guidance Works

What happens in a session — and what you can expect

Before We Begin

Wherever you are, you are welcome here.

Every person who comes to Booska Spiritual Guidance arrives at a different place in their journey. Some are searching for direction. Some are walking through heartbreak or loss. Some simply want to know God more deeply.

Sessions are prayerful, one-on-one conversations led by the Holy Spirit. Cindy listens — both to you and to the Lord — and shares what she discerns with care, humility, and biblical grounding.

1

Book Your Session

Choose a session type and select a date and time that works for you. You will receive a confirmation and any preparation notes by email. Payment is made securely online at the time of booking.

2

Prepare Your Heart

Before your session, take a few moments to pray and bring to mind the questions or areas of your life you would most like to bring before God. There is no script required — simply come with an open heart.

3

We Seek God Together

In a warm, confidential, one-on-one setting, Cindy will open in prayer and prayerfully listen for the Holy Spirit's direction. She will share what she discerns, offer biblical encouragement, and provide space for you to respond, ask questions, and process.

4

Receive and Reflect

After your session, take time to pray over what was shared. Test every word against Scripture and your own relationship with God. Cindy's desire is never to replace the Holy Spirit — but to help you recognize His voice more clearly in your own life.

Sessions Are Designed For
  • Seeking direction for a major life decision
  • Processing grief, loss, or a difficult season
  • Desiring breakthrough in your prayer life
  • Growing in confidence in hearing God's voice
  • Finding renewed purpose and spiritual clarity
  • Strengthening your relationship with Jesus Christ
What This Is Not
  • Psychic or intuitive readings
  • Divination, tarot, astrology, or mediumship
  • Fortune-telling or predicting the future
  • Communication with spirits of the deceased
  • A replacement for your relationship with God

FAQ

Questions are welcome here

Honest answers about what Booska Spiritual Guidance is — and what it is not.

What is Booska Spiritual Guidance?

+

Booska Spiritual Guidance is a Spirit-led mentoring ministry rooted in biblical Christian faith. Cindy Booska prayerfully listens for the Holy Spirit's direction and offers biblical encouragement, discernment, and spiritual mentoring to those seeking a deeper relationship with God.

Is this the same as a psychic reading?

+

No — not at all. Cindy's guidance is grounded entirely in Scripture and the Holy Spirit. She does not practice divination, fortune-telling, mediumship, tarot, astrology, or communication with spirits of the deceased. If you are searching for biblical, Spirit-led encouragement, you are in the right place.

Do I need to be a Christian to book a session?

+

All are welcome. Cindy's heart is to help people encounter the love of Jesus Christ — wherever they are on their journey. Whether you are a long-time believer or simply curious about God, you are welcome to reach out.

What happens in a session?

+

Sessions are prayerful, one-on-one conversations. Cindy will open in prayer, listen for the Holy Spirit's direction, and share what she discerns with biblical grounding and care. You are welcome to ask questions, share what is on your heart, and respond freely. Sessions are completely confidential.

How do I prepare for my session?

+

Come as you are. If helpful, take a few moments before your session to pray and bring to mind the areas of your life you would most like to bring before God. There is no script — simply come with an open and willing heart.

Will I receive a recording or notes?

+

Sessions are private and are not recorded without your consent. Where led by the Spirit, Cindy may offer brief written encouragement notes following your session.

What is your cancellation policy?

+

Please provide at least 48 hours notice to cancel or reschedule your session. Cancellations made with 48 hours or more notice will receive a full refund. Cancellations made with less than 48 hours notice are non-refundable.

How do I know if what Cindy shares is from God?

+

Cindy encourages every person to test what they receive through prayer, Scripture, and their own relationship with God. As 1 Thessalonians 5:21 says — test everything, hold on to what is good. Her desire is never to replace the Holy Spirit but to help you recognize His voice more clearly in your own life.

How do I pay?

+

Payment is made securely online at the time of booking via Stripe (all major credit and debit cards accepted). Sessions are confirmed upon receipt of payment.

Can I share a testimonial?

+

Cindy would be deeply grateful. If a session has been meaningful to you, you are welcome to reach out through the Contact page to share your experience. Your story may encourage someone else who is on the same journey.
